\section{Exercise eleven}

Consider now the code:
\begin{verbnobox}[\verbarg]
ADDI S3, S2, 2
ADD S5, S4, S3
SW S5, 4(S3)
SUB S7, S5, S6
LS S6, 4(S7)
\end{verbnobox}
