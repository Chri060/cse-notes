\begin{abstract}
    The course topics are: 
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Basic concepts of stochastic processes.
        \item ARMA and ARMAX classes of parametric models for time series and for Input/Output systems.
        \item Parameter identification of ARMA and ARMAX models.
        \item Analysis of identification methods.
        \item Model validation and pre-processing.
    \end{itemize}
\end{abstract}
